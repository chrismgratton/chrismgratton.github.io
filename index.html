How to Play Solitaire

The Tableau

    • There are 7 piles of cards.
    • The first card in each pile is face up.
    • The rest of the cards are face down.

The Foundation Piles

  • There are 4 piles of cards.
  • The cards are built up in suit, from ace to king.

The Stock Pile

  • A pile of cards that is face down.
  • You can turn over the top card of the stock pile.

The Waste Pile

  • A pile of cards that are face up.
  • Cards from the waste pile can be moved to the tableau or the foundation piles.

How To Move Cards

  • Move: Move a card to a pile if it is one rank higher than the card on top and of a different color.
  • King: Move a king to an empty space.
  • Stock: If you can't move any cards, turn over the top card of the stock pile.
  • Waste: You can move cards from the waste pile to the tableau or the foundation piles.
  • Aces: Aces can be moved to the foundation piles as soon as they are exposed, regardless of their color.

How to Win

  • The game is won when all the cards are moved to the foundation piles.
